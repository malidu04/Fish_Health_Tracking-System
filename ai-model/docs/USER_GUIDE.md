## AI Disease Prediction

### How It Works
1. The AI analyzes symptom patterns
2. Compares with known disease profiles
3. Provides confidence scores and recommendations
4. Learns from your treatment outcomes over time

### Common Diseases Detected
- **Ich (White Spot Disease)**: White spots, loss of appetite
- **Fin Rot**: Deteriorating fins, lesions
- **Dropsy**: Bloating, lethargy, pineconing scales
- **Velvet Disease**: Gold dust appearance, flashing
- **Swim Bladder Disorder**: Buoyancy issues, abnormal swimming

## Best Practices

### Regular Maintenance
1. **Daily**: Feed appropriate amounts, observe behavior
2. **Weekly**: Test water parameters, partial water changes
3. **Monthly**: Deep clean, equipment maintenance

### Health Monitoring
1. **Observe Behavior**: Watch for changes in swimming, eating, social behavior
2. **Check Physical Condition**: Look for color changes, lesions, abnormal growths
3. **Monitor Water Quality**: Regular testing prevents many health issues

### Quarantine Procedures
1. Always quarantine new fish for 2-4 weeks
2. Use separate equipment for quarantine tanks
3. Monitor closely for symptoms before introducing to main tank

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues
1. **Fish Not Eating**:
   - Check water parameters
   - Try different foods
   - Observe for other symptoms

2. **Cloudy Water**:
   - Test water parameters
   - Check filtration
   - Reduce feeding

3. **Aggressive Behavior**:
   - Ensure adequate space
   - Check compatibility
   - Provide hiding places

### Emergency Procedures
1. **Isolate**: Move sick fish to quarantine immediately
2. **Test Water**: Check ammonia, nitrite, nitrate levels
3. **Water Change**: Perform 25-50% water change
4. **Consult**: Use AI recommendations or seek expert advice
